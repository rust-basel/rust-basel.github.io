Rust is known for being applicable to a many different levels of programming, but one exciting area that Rust opens up is embedded computing. In this fun workshop we got a
taste of working with microcontrollers using Rust. We were generously sponsored so we could provide [ESP32 microcontrollers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ESP32) to participants.
Participants learned how to

- connect to the microcontroller and flash it over USB
- connect the microcontroller to simple electronics
- interface with sensors
- buddy a microcontroller with a lightweight SoC
